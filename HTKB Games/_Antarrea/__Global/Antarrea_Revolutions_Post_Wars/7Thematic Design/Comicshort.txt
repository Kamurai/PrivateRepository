Comic Book World:

Psychodom - Worldwide empire controlling, officially, all lands, seas and space immediately surrounding the planet up to and including the planet's moon.

Interstellar - Agency observing/guarding the planet from orbital stations.
		Chiefly an intelligence agency.

Enforcement - Soldiers that uphold the law and act as street judges.

Extraction - Recruiting forces of soldiers:  sent after SPBs.

Elimination - Seek and destroy faction:  sent after uncooperative SPBs.
