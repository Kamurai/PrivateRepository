Sacred Offerings

Setup:
	Select a board for the number of players:
		The two player board is a square with two alters.
		The three player board is a triangle.
		The four player board is a centric square.
		The five player board is a pentagon.
		The six player board is a hexagon.
		
Player turn:
	Each turn a player must move a character on the board.
	Characters move in one of two ways:
		Like a King in chess, one space in any direction.
		Like a King in checkers, able to jump multiple pieces.
	That character must end closer to the alter than they began.
	If a player CAN sacrifice a character, they must.
	
Scoring:
	A player gets +1 point on a turn they did not sacrifice a character.
	A player get -x points equal to the value of the character sacrificed.
	
End condition:
	No characters are left on the board.

Victory condition:
	The player with the higher points wins!
