Conquer Ruleset v0.1

Setup:
	Board:
		Place each Kaiju on the center of the outmost layer of each sector
		Place defenses on civilian territories
		Place gold in civilian territories
	Kaiju cards:
		Place defense charges on the cards
		Place attack charges on the cards
		

Round:
	Each player spends their alloted number of action points.

Turn:
	A Kaiju gets 3 (base) actions:
		
		Move to an adjacent territory
		Score Victory Points
		Attack a territory
		Play a card
		Charge Defense
		Get a discount
		Charge attack

End Condition:
	At the end of the final round, the game ends.

Win Condition:
	At the end of the final round, the Kaiju with the most accumulated points, wins.
