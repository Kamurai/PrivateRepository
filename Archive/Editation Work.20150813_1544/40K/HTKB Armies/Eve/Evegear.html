Eve Wargear Page


Wargear

Broadsword: Single handed close combat weapon.

Claymore: Two handed close combat weapon. Gives user +1 to Strength.

WarTalons: These count as 2 single handed close combat weapons.

Master-Crafted Weapons: A Master-Crafted Weapon may re-roll a missed hit (you cannot Master Craft Grenades). If you Master Craft a weapon it's 15 points extra, but does not count towards the 100 point limit on Wargear.

Force Staff: Treated as a Power Weapon, but can only be equipped by Mages/Sages. Roll to hit, to wound and save as normal. If one wound is inflicted, you can make a Psychic test, and if passed (normal rules applied), then the opponent is killed outright. You cannot use another Psychic Power the same turn as this. This does not exclude Eve ballisically fired psychic abilities.

Power Claw: Counts as a power weapon.

Eve Shot weapons: These are shoulder mounted and do not count towards the maximum numbers of weapons available to carry. However, any one model may have only one shot weapon.

Diresword: Two handed power weapon. Gives +1 Strength.

Death Scythe: Force weapon, but is always allow to kill outright.

Razor Talons: Two single handed power weapons, re-roll each roll to wound.

Great Talons: Two single handed power weapons, re-roll each roll to hit.

Wings: Allows the model to make a 12" movement and ignore difficult terrain while moving, but may not land in difficult terrain.
