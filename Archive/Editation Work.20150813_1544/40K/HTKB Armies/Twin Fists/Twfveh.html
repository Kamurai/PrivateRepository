Marines of the Twin Fists Vehicle Upgrade Page


Vehicle Upgrades
Extra Armour: Changes any Crew Stunned results to Crew Shaken.

Searchlight: Only used for games with Night Fighting rules. it allows an enemy unit spotted by the vehicle to be shot at by the rest of your army. A vehicle with a searchlight is suseptable to enemy fire.

Smoke Launchers: These are used once per battle. They can be used any turn, regardless of movement, but cannot shoot during the same turn they are used. Any Penetrating hits are counted as Glancing hits when concealed, this only lasts one turn.

Dozer Blade: Allows a vehicle to re-roll a failed Difficult Terrain Test if it did not move more than 6" that turn.

Razor Treads: Any enemy model making a Close Combat Attack on the Vehicle suffers a Strength D6 and Armour Piercing 4 hit. When a vehicle makes a "Tank Shock" and the enemy units don't fall back, roll a D6 for each model forced to move out of the way and on a D6 roll of 4+. Armour Saves are taken as normal.
