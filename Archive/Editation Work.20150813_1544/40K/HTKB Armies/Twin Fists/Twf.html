HQsThese are the generals of your army, chose whom you deem worthy of the position.
Hereos: The Hero is the backbone of leadership in your army, allow him to charge into battle independently, or accompanied by a Gloryguard.
Blackbelt: A master gone corrupt, the Blackbelt is a Sune Martial Artist and he will charge into combat full bore.
Gloryguard: A Gloryguard can accompany any other HQ character.
Sune Mage: A master in the art of the mind in fighting, the Sune Mage can call upon the powers of war to do devastating damage to its enemies.


Twinfists Special Characters: These are the unique characters in the story lines of WARHAMMER40K.


Troops: These are the cannon fodder of your army, defending their emporer to the last shot fired through their skulls.
Foot Soldiers: Grown in labs, and developed with super human armor, these troops will fill in spaces in your units. They attack using Chains and Claws.
Samurai: These slightly stronger units with Kotachis, and can come in some-what larger numbers.


Transports: These tanks are used to carry units into the fray quicker than they normally could on their own.
Jackal: This tank carries many men, but does not have any grand weaponry.


Elites: These men are the best of the best because they've out lived most others, most are costly and should be used wisely.
War Terminators: These units are made up of some of the strongest men in your army, though costly, are well worth their price.
High Priests: An upgrade of Twin Fist Foot Soldiers, these High Priests fight with Chains and Katanas.
Grand Monks: An upgrade of Twin Fist Samurai, these Grand Monks are trained heavily in the use of the Kotachi.


Fast Attack: These highly mobile units are used to speed up your army's attack.
Eagle Claws: These Space Marines are ready to jump into the frey, wearing jump packs, they can move farther than normal troops.
Monk Riders Squadron: These Space Marines ride bike that allow them to move farther, and rush into combat.
Ninja: These Space Marines are ready to jump into the frey, wearing jump packs, they can move farther than normal troops, but also use Shurikens.

Heavy Support: These units are the heavy fighters of the army.
Twinfist Dreadnought: The Twinfist Dreadnought uses its four arms to pummel the enemy to death.
Marauders: A smaller version of a Dreadnought (technically), the Marauders use their four arms to attack their enemies.
