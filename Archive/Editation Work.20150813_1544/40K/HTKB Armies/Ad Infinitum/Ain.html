HQs These are the generals of your army, chose whom you deem worthy of the position.

Command Unit : This unit contains the best units available to the Ad Infinitum. This is the core base for all of the units within the army. .

Rogue Unit : A Rogue is a four-armed beast of a machine, which is designed specifically for hand to hand combat.



Ad Infinitum Special CharactersThese are the unique characters in the story lines of WARHAMMER40K.


Troops: These are the cannon fodder of your army, defending their emporer to the last shot fired through their skulls.

Nale Unit: This unit is as basic as you get. However, Nale units are as strong as a Space Marine, quick as an Eldar guardian, and as 'ardy as a Nob.

Galis Unit: The Galis unit is a Nale unit equip with Power Sabres. Goodbye armor saves.

Titan Unit: The Titan unit is a Nale unit equip with Power Fists. Goobye pesky vehicles.


Transports: These tanks are used to carry units into the fray quicker than they normally could on their own.

Elites: These men are the best of the best because they've out lived most others, most are costly and should be used wisely.

Tacyon Unit: An upgraded version of the Galis Unit, this unit includes Fusion Pistols in the design.

Akira Gear: Basically a large version of the Tacyon, this gear is fast and brutal.



Fast Attack: These highly mobile units are used to speed up your army's attack.

Aquis: An upgraded version of the Nale Unit, this unit is fast and equip with Auto Pisotls.

Lisma: A faster version of the Akira gear, this gear can move twice as far.



Heavy Support: These units are the big guns of the army, reaking havoc from the back lines, they use powerful weapons and awesome fighting strength to attack.

Hurd: A Hurd Unit is fastened with only one type of ranged weapon and is designed to be deadly accurate.

Mirv: A Mirv Unit is sometimes used for Heavy Support apart from the HQ position.
