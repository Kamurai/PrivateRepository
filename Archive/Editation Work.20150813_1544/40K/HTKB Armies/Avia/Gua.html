Numbers: The Guardian Squad has 3-8 of Guardians, plus an Overtaker, and a Seraphim.

Weapons: Each Guardian is equipped with a Lasgun or Laspistol and a Close Combat Weapon.

Options: Up two Guardians may have weapons other than a Lasgun or a Laspistol and Close Combat Weapon.
One Guardian with a Lasgun may have a Photon Cannon, +15 points.
Up to two Guardians with a Lasgun may have a Photon Rifle, +4 points, Plasmagun, +6 points, Flamer, +3 points, or a Meltagun, +8 points.
Up to two Guardians with a Laspistol and Close Combat Weapon may have a Photon Pistol, +3 points, or a Plasma Pistol, +5 points.

Special Rules: All Avia may ignore difficult terrain while moving.
