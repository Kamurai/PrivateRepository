Ded Setzer's Avia Page


HQsThese are the generals of your army, chose whom you deem worthy of the position.

Seraphim: The organized leader of the Angelic Race, this unit can be accompanied by a Guardian Squad.

Angel: A rogue, this leader flies into combat alone to fight and die in a glorious battle.

Guardian Squad: A squad of elite Avia, these units are simple back up for the Seraphim.

Overwatcher: A chosen few are picked by the Seraphim to lead each squad. Every squad of Avia has an Overwatcher leading it.



Avia Special CharactersThese are the unique characters in the story lines of WARHAMMER40K.


Troops: These are the cannon fodder of your army, defending their emporer to the last shot fired through their skulls.

Herald Squad: Experience fliers, these small bands of light scouts can move around the board at will.

Warrior Squad: Trained in the art of battle, these units use their weapons in the most lethal manner.

Voices Squad: Roaming in large numbers thes units take out enemy squad by pure numbers.

Sirenth Squad: These units are seemingly impossibly fast, and attack like lightning.

Decker Squad: These Avia have advance technology at their disposle and abuse it at their will.


Transports: These tanks are used to carry units into the fray quicker than they normally could on their own.

Elites: These men are the best of the best because they've out lived most others, most are costly and should be used wisely.

Seraph Squad: Having been through constant war in their life, these Avia have built a toughness for battle.

Prophets Squad: The only explanation for the Prophets speed is that they can see the future, and can react to it before their enemies.

Serra Squad: Living in the sacred palaces of weaponry, the Serra are a sever threat of combat.

Techno-Rangers: The more elite techno-wizs of the Avia, these fire arm specialists are a powerful force.



Fast Attack: These highly mobile units are used to speed up your army's attack.

Acolytes Squad: These enhanced warriors are filled with adreniline by artificial inducers upon sight of an enemy.

Wraith Squad: With slightly stronger wings than the rest of the Avia, these units are extremely mobile.

Oracle Squad: Thought to be able to see terrain as gods, the Oracles are uninhibited by fear of the enemy.

Speedware Squad: Avia with mechanically enhanced wings, these units move in and shoot 'em up fast.



Heavy Support: These units are the big guns of the army, reaking havoc from the back lines, they use powerful weapons and awesome fighting strength to attack.

Paladins: Paladins pride themselves in the use of ancient techniques of the sword and shield, allowing fate to intervene when neccessary.

Dragoons: Dragoons keep track of the kills from each battle and compare for a prize after the battle.

Artificers: Almost crazed Avia, these units charge into combat full-bore, believing that the world shall end in hand to hand.

Hardware Squad: Loaded down with lots of heavy weaponry, these Avia are trained to eliminate the enemy.
