Boom Weapons: Usuable in an assault, or during shooting phase. Roll a D6 against the model's BS. If passed, it explodes with appropriate Template. If failed roll a D6:
1)Explodes before rest of unit (yes it can hurt your models) with a Ordnance Template
2)Explode before res of unit (yes it can hurt your models) with a Blast Template
3)Scatters D6 inches and explodes (on time with unit)
4)It DIES. (yep, shrivels up and burns out)
5 or 6)It explodes on time.

Spore Cloud: If a Spore Cannon is manned by Weedlings, they can direct it, and fire by measuring to a target within 48" and line of sight, then roll a Scatter D6 and place the Ordnance Template that far from the target.
If not manned by Weedlings, the Spore Cannon takes a Guess 48" shot.

Close Combat Weapon: Roll to wound with user's Strength, and opponent make take Saves as normal.

Venom Weapons: Roll to wound with user's Strength, and ignore opponent's Save.

Super Weapons: The user may double its Strength up to a max of 10 when rolling to wound, and ignore opponent's Save. These always strike last.
