Ded Setzer's Voboulids Page


HQsThese are the generals of your army, chose whom you deem worthy of the position.

Spore Queen: Every major Garden is led by a single Spore Queen, and every other plant in its Garden seems to show a vague respect and devotion to the Spore Queen.

Juvenile Queen: Sometimes a smaller Garden is headed by one or two Juvenile Queen, when the grow larger, they'll start to fight for dominance, then eat the loser.

Oculars: Strange, but incredibly intelligent plants, these have been known to fry opponents into thin air by focusing their large "eye" on them and using the refracted light as a weapon.



Voboulids Special CharactersThese are the unique characters in the story lines of WARHAMMER40K.


Troops: These are the cannon fodder of your army, defending their emporer to the last shot fired through their skulls.

Melta-Pods: These Plants are usually dormant, but can direct themselves in times of need. Upon sighting or contact of an unfriendly thing, they tend to explode.

Writhers: An unintellible mass of vines and other obscure plant parts, these Plants are usually found in large numbers.

Micro-Tarvandars: Resembling a large, walking, Venus-Fly-Trap, these plants use their massive jaws to drain enemies of fluid.

Blood Vines: Weed-like creatures, they spring from the ground, entwining themselves around their enemies.


Transports: These tanks are used to carry units into the fray quicker than they normally could on their own.

Elites: These men are the best of the best because they've out lived most others, most are costly and should be used wisely.

Garden Walkers: Extremely evovled plant-life, these bi-pedal weeds actually use biological Thorn Rifles in addition to vicious fighting tactics.

Tarvanders: An Venu-Fly-Trap about the size of a pair of Rhinos, it attacks with it's vicious, massive jaws, draining the victim of its fluids.

Tangler: A some-what evolved form of a Writher, these Plant masses come in mass numbers and are slightly more vicious.



Fast Attack: These highly mobile units are used to speed up your army's attack.

Sink-Pods: A variation of a Melta-Pod, these pods are more hyper-active and tend to move a great deal faster.

Locusts: A super-evolve weed, these Plants resemble Locusts, and use biological Thorn Rifles.

Stick-Weeds: Strange creatures, they strike from the ground with stiff stalk and dangerous vines.



Heavy Support: These units are the big guns of the army, reaking havoc from the back lines, they use powerful weapons and awesome fighting strength to attack.

Spore Cannons: These massive Pods launch storms of acid spore, and are guard by masses of small venomous weeds.

Grundler: Large stupid plants, these are comprable to the Juvenile Queen in strength, but are comparable to dirt in intelligence.

Boom-Shrooms: Similar to a large walking mushroom, this plant with explode much like a Melta-Pod, only to an extreme.
