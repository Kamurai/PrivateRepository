Volboulids Wargear Page


Wargear

Carapace: This increases the model's Armor Save by 1 (i.e. from 5+ to 4+).

Rejuvenation: If a model with Rejuvenation is killed, place the model on its side, and roll a D6 at the start of the next turn. On a D6 roll of 6 the model is stood back up with 1 wound, and on any other roll is taken as a causuality.

Acid Sacs: These give a model a 2D6 roll for armour penetration in addition to its Strength.

Pituitary Glands: Pituitary Glands allow a 12" move per turn, and ignore difficult terrain. A character can only use a Jump Pack if the rest of the units in a squad are also equipped with Pituitary Glands. Characters with Wings may use Deep Strike rules.

Adrenlines: Adrenline Sacs allow a model to re-roll a missed hit (in Close Combat).

Seed Bombs: Seed Bombs give -1 to the Initiative of a squad initiatiing combat with the model during the first round of combat.
