Vehicle: The Dreadnought is a Walker of one Crew.

Numbers: Dreadnoughts are one per unit.

Weapons: A GeoMarine Dreadnought is armed with a Powerfist with a built-in Storm Lit Bolter, and a Twin-Linked Heavy Lit Bolter.

Options: The GeoMarine Dreadnought may have any of the following vehicle upgrades: Extra Armour, +5 points, Searchlight, +1 point, or Smoke Launchers, +3 points.
The Twin-Linked Heavy Lit Bolter can be upgraded to one of the following: PH Cannon, +35 points, Twin-Linked Lascannon, +50 points, Multi-Melta, +40 points, or Missile Launcher, +45 points.
The Storm Lit Bolter can be upgraded to a Heavy Flamer, +10 points.
