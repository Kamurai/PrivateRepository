Numbers: A Wind Riders squad consists of 5-10 Wind Riders.

Weapons: Each Wind Rider is equipped with a Lit Bolt Pistol, and Close Combat Weapon.

Options: One model may be a Sergeant, that is equipped with a Twin-Linked Lit Bolt Pistol and Power Weapon.
Up to two Wind Riders may be equipped with: a Flamer, +5 points, an Acidgun, +15 points, a Meltagun, +10, or a Storm Lit Bolter, +15.

Special Rules: Wind Riders may use Deep Strike rules.
