Numbers: A GeoMarine Terminator squad consists of 5-10 Terminators.

Any Terminator may upgrade his Powerfist with a Chainfist, +5 points.
Up to one Terminator may have a Heavy Flamer, +10 points, PH Cannon, +25 points, or Multi-Melta, +20 points.
Any Terminator may change their weapons for a pair of Lightning Claws, or a Thunder Hammer and Storm Shield.

Special Rules: Terminators may use Deep Strike rules.
