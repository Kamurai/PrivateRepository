Rapid Fire Weapons:

A Rapid Fire 1 weapon shoots 1 shot at full length and 2 at half length when standing still, and when moving 1 shot at half length.

A Rapid Fire 2 weapon shoots 2 shots at full length and 4 at half length when standing still, and when moving 1 shot at full length, 2 at half length.

A Rapid Fire 3 weapon shoots 3 shots at full length and 6 at half length when standing still, and when moving 1 shot at full length, 2 at 2/3 length, and 3 at 1/3 length.

Close Combat Weapon: Roll to wound with user's Strength, and opponent make take Saves as normal.

Power Weapon: Roll to wound with user's Strenght, and ignore opponent's Save.

Power Fist: The user may double its Strength up to a max of 10 when rolling to wound, and ignore opponent's Save. These always strike last.

Dreadnought Close Combat Weapons: The user may double its Strength up to a max of 10 when rolling to wound, and ignore opponent's Save. These do not have to strike last.
