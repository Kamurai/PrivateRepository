Numbers: Overtakers are one per Unit.

Weapons: An Overtaker is armed with four Powerfists. An Overtaker also has Heavy Lit Bolter, a PH Cannon, a Heavy Flamer, a Missile Launcher, and a Lascannon.

Options: The GeoMarine Dreadnought may have any of the following vehicle upgrades: Extra Armour, +5 points, Searchlight, +1 point, or Smoke Launchers, +3 points.

Special Rules: THERE CAN BE ONLY ONE - An Overtaker will hunt out opposing HQs first. Each turn, check its reference to opposing HQ units. The Overtaker can only move if it can not fire at an opposing HQ unit, and then it must move towards the enemy HQ unit (i.e. in a direction of 1o-179o, when the oposing HQ is at 90o.) If the Overtaker is STILL not in line of sight or range of an opposing HQ, then it may fire at any enemy unit within line of sight or range.
