Numbers: The Geo Guard Squad consists of 4-9 Geo Guards.

Weapons: Each Geo Guard is equipped with a Lit Bolter, or two Lit Bolt Pistols.

Options: The Geo Guard Squad can include one Veteran Honor Sergeant which may have any have any equipment from the GeoMarine Armoury. If you choice not to equip the Veteran Honor Sergeant with ANYTHING from the Armoury, he has a Photon Pistol and Power Weapon.

Special Rules: *If the squad is led by an HQ with Terminator Honours, then the rest of the squad may have Terminator Honours, +10 points per model*.
A Veteran Honor Sergeant may act as an HQ, but is not a Independent Character, therefore it must be accompanied by its Geo Guard.
If the squad is led by an HQ unit with a Jump Pack, Wind Riders must be used for Geo Guard, except the Veteran Honor Sergeant. The Veteran Honor Sergeant must be given a Jump Pack seperately.
One member of the Geo Guard can become a Standard Bearer. If the Standard Bearer has a flag, then instead of a Geo Guard, a corresponding unit must be used.
