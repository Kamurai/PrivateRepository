HQsThese are the generals of your army, chose whom you deem worthy of the position.
Hereos: The Hero is the backbone of leadership in your army, allow him to charge into battle independently, or accompanied by a Command Squad.
Overtaker: An Overtaker is rightly named, from a standing position, it's equipped to take on any opponent.
Geoguard: A Geoguard can accompany any other HQ character.
GeoMarine Special Characters: These are the unique characters in the story lines of WARHAMMER40K.
Troops: These are the cannon fodder of your army, defending their emporer to the last shot fired through their skulls.
Gunners: Grown in labs, and developed with super human armor, these troops will fill in spaces by specializing in artillery warfare.
Specialists: Specialists are actually hand to hand specialists, needing to get up in the action before becoming effective.
Elites: These men are the best of the best because they've out lived most others, most are costly and should be used wisely.
Flamer Marine Squad: These Elites are trained heavily with Flame Throwers, and know how to be deadly with them.
Tank Marine Squad: These Elites are trained heavily with anit-tank weapons, making them a great asset to vehicular destruction.
Aqua Marine Squad: These Elites are trained heavily with acid spewing weaponry, letting you eat through the compition.
GeoMarine Veteran Squad: Best of the best, they prove it by being there.
Fast Attack: These highly mobile units are used to speed up your army's attack.
Wind Rider Squad: These Space Marines are ready to jump into the frey, wearing jump packs, they can move farther than normal troops.
GeoBike Squadron: These Space Marines ride bike that allow them to move farther, and rush into combat.
Geo Assault Bike: These Space Marines ride bikes that are good for getting quick range with a heavy weapons side-cart.
Heavy Support: These units are the big guns of the army, reaking havoc from the back lines, they use powerful long range weapons to attack.
GeoMarine Dreadnought: This Dreadnought allows a variety of heavy weapons fire, allong with the deadly art of hand to hand combat..
Devastator Squad: This is an unit that can carry up to four heavy weapons with them.
Terminators: These units are made up of some of the strongest men in your army, though costly, are wealth worth their price.
Unnofficial Vehicle Support: These are Proto-type Tanks made for Transport, or for wicked fire power.
Geo Marines Proto-Type: Bad Moon: A tank design for VERY heavy firing.
Geo Marines Proto-Type: Composter: A rolling microwave of destruction.
Geo Marines Proto-Type: Deposler: Desgined with the PH Cannon, this tank will eat at any other vehicle
Geo Marines Proto-Type: Hellwalker: Combines great armour and the art of the Flamethrower.
Geo Marines Proto-Type: Ivory Horn: Designed for cheap transport and a gunner tank.
