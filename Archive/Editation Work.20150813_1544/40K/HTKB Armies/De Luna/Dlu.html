HQsThese are the generals of your army, chose whom you deem worthy of the position.

Alpha: The king of gears, the Alpha gear armed like a dreadnought, and looks like a wraithlord, this gear is ready for any situation.

Beta: The Beta gear is design for melee attacks, similarly to the Blood Angels Dreadnought Furioso.

Omega: A mysteriously new design of gear, the Omega gear is design for precision ranged firing.

Delta: Much smaller versions of the Omega gears, the Delta gears are designed to support the HQ.



De Luna Special CharactersThese are the unique characters in the story lines of WARHAMMER40K.


Troops: These are the cannon fodder of your army, defending their emporer to the last shot fired through their skulls.

Unum: The Unum gear is the gear most similar to the Imperial Dreadnoughts.

Decem: Similar to the Delta gear, the Decem gear is designed more for stand alone fighting.

Centum: The Centum gear is the basic melee gladiator of the De Luna army.


Transports: These tanks are used to carry units into the fray quicker than they normally could on their own.

Elites: These men are the best of the best because they've out lived most others, most are costly and should be used wisely.

Gamma: A slightly faster version of the Decem gear, the Gamma gear can move twice as far.

Epsilon: A slightly faster version of the Centum gear, the Epsilon gear can move twice as far.

Kappa: A slightly faster version of the Unum gear, the Kappa gear can move twice as far.



Fast Attack: These highly mobile units are used to speed up your army's attack.

Sigma: An even faster version of the Unum gear, the Sigma gear can move four times as far.

Pi: An even faster version of the Decem gear, the Pi gear can move four times as far.

Mu: An even faster version of the Centum gear, the Mu gear can move four times as far.



Heavy Support: These units are the big guns of the army, reeking havoc from the back lines, they use powerful weapons and awesome fighting strength to attack.

Zeta: Made for taking the heat, the Zeta gear is a stronger and more heavily armored version of Centum gear.

Phi: Made for taking the heat, the Phi gear is a stronger and more heavily armored version of Decem gear.

Xena: Made for taking the heat, the Xena gear is a stronger and more heavily armored version of Unum gear.
