Space Marines Wargear Page


Wargear

Articifer Armour: This armour give a 2+ Armour Save, but cannot be used with items that are "Terminators only".

Auspex: This is a short range scanner to detect hidden troops. If enemy infiltrators set up within 4D6" of a model with an Auspex, then the model is allowed a free shot or sound the alarm (Raid Scenario). If the model is part of a unit, then the whole unit may shoot before the game begins, normal shooting rules apply and this can cause Infiltrators to fall back.

Bionics: If a model with Bionics is killed, place the model on its side, and roll a D6 at the start of the next turn. On a D6 roll of 6 the model is stood back up with 1 wound, and on any other roll is taken as a causuality.

Chainfist: This gives a Powerfist a 2D6 roll for armour penetration.

Chapter Banner: If revealed (once per battle), all Space Marines within 2D6" get a +1 Attack till end of turn, and add +1 to any Space Marine Close Combat that takes place within 6" of the Chapter Banner. If the model with a Chapter Banner is slain in Close Combat, then the enemy model gets that slew it gets the Chapter Banner. It can change hands serval times, as long as the model holding it is slain each time.

Combi-Weapons: This allows a Marine to fire either a Bolter (may be fired multiple times) or a special weapon (can only be fired once per battle), but not both at once.

Crozius Arcanium: A Chaplain's Power Weapon.

Cyclone Missile Launcher: A Terminator Missile Launcher that replaces the Terminator's Close Combat Weapon.

Force Weapon: Treated as a Power Weapon, but can only be equipped by Librarians. Roll to hit, to wound and save as normal. If one wound is inflicted, you can make a Psychic test, and if passed (normal rules applied), then the opponent is killed outright. You cannot use another Psychic Power the same turn as this.

Holy Relic: This be revealed once per battle, when revealed all Space Marines within 2D6" receive +1 Attack.

Iron Halo: This gives a Space Marine a 4+ invulnerable save as an option to its normal Armour Save. Only one of these are allowed per army, and cannot be given to a Marine with Terminator Armour or a Rosarius.

Jump Packs: Jump Packs allow a 12" move per turn, and ignore difficult terrain. A character can only use a Jump Pack if the rest of the units in a squad are also equipped with Jump Packs. Characters with Jump Packs may use Deep Strike rules.

Lightning Claws: Count as a Power Weapon, and may re-roll failed wounds. Models armed with a Lightning Claw only get a +1 Attack modifier for an additional Close Combat Weapon if the 2nd weapon is another Lightning Claw.

Master-Crafted Weapons: A Master-Crafted Weapon may re-roll a missed hit (you cannot Master Craft Grenades). If you Master Craft a weapon it's 15 points extra, but does not count towards the 100 point limit on Wargear.

Narthecium: Apothecary item. It allows you to ignore the first failed saving throw each turn for the Apothecary's unit, but not wounds from "Instant Death" or that ignores Armour Saves completely or if the Apothecary is in base to base contact.

Psychic Hood: Librarian item. Declare use of the Psychic Hood after a successful Psychic attack, but before the power is used. Each player rolls a D6 + Leadership, if Psychic Hood wearer scores lower, the attack is nulled, but if the score is equal to or higher than the opponent's, then the suffer the Psychic Attack.

Purity Seals: The unit in which a model with Purity Seals is grouped with may rolls an extra D6 for falling back, and dismiss one. The unit falls back the remain amount of D6"

Reductor: Apothecary item. If the Apothecary is alive at the end of the game, you get +1 victory point for each of your dead guys.

Rosarius: Chaplain item. Allows a 4+ Invulnerable Save, as an option to the normal save.

Sacred Standard: Add +1 wound to the combat resolution of the unit with 6" of the Sacred Standard. If the model with the Sacred Standard is killed, then the enemy model that killed it gains the Sacred Standard for itself. As long as the holder of the Sacred Standard is slain each time the Sacred Standard can switch possession many times.

Servo-Arm: Techmarine item. Can be used at the beginning of any turn to repair an immobilised vehicle in base contact with the Techmarine by rolling a 6 on a D6. In combat, the Servo-Arm is treated as a Powerfist and always hits on a 4+, this is rolled seperately from the Techmarine's attacks.

Signum: Techmarine item. Allows you to re-roll one missed shot for the Command Squad that the Techmarine is in.

Space Marine Bike: This is equipped with Twin-Linked Bolters and give a Toughness bonus of +1. If not an Independent Character, the character being given a Bike can only have it if the rest of the squad is equipped with Bikes.

Storm Shield: This allows an optional 4+ Invulnerable Save in Close Combat against one opponent. Ranged attacks are unaffected by this item, and it cannot be used in combination with a Rosarius or Iron Halo.

Teleport Homer: This will allows a unit Deep Striking to not scatter upon teleportation, but only works with teleportation, not those that are dropped.

Terminator Armour: This allows a character to move and shoot with Heavy Weapons, but restricts the character's ability to advance, instead it can only consolidate. The Terminator Armour give a 2+ Save and adds +1 Attack, it also allows the character to be teleported onto the battlefield using Deep Strike rules.

Terminator Honours: This gives a +1 Attack, which is already included in charateristics of Veteran Sergeants and any model that is equipped with Terminator Armour by default.

Thunder Hammmer: Counts as a Powerfist, but an opponent wounded by it and not killed may not attack until the end of the next assault phase.
